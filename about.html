About Us

Zaynah Fashions is a Johannesburg based Fashion brand that curates Indian fashion and jewellery since 2022. We offer a versatile range of handcrafted and modern-crafted ethnic designs. Zaynah Fashions translates to the process of beautification and adornment. Our tagline is “your one stop shop for ethnic clothing” is based on the role fashion plays in enhancing a person’s confidence and sense of self. We wish to create an unforgettable impression through the fashion we curate.
